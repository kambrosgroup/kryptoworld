Wallets

  • Crypto Wallet Types Explained: Hot vs Cold Storage

    February 23, 2026 · 6 min read

    “Not your keys, not your coins.” You’ll hear this phrase constantly in crypto circles. It sounds cryptic, but it’s actually simple: if you don’t control the private keys to your crypto, you don’t really own it. This guide explains your storage options—from leaving crypto on exchanges (convenient but risky) to hardware wallets (secure but cost money). By the end, you’ll know exactly how to store your crypto based on how much you own and your risk tolerance.
